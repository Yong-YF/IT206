Current Situation


The rapid urbanization of human society, the increased production of processed foods and drink led to the change of human lifestyles. This cause the shift in our dietary patterns. Unhealthy diet and lack of physical activities are leading global risks to health. The unbalanced diet such as the consuming food in high fats, free sugars and salts and lack in fibers from fruits, vegetables and the whole grains will come with the malnutrition such as noncommunicable diseases (NCDs),including such as diabetes, heart disease, stroke and cancer.

In 2016, more than 1.9 billion adults, 18 years and older, were overweight. Of these over 650 million were obese. Most of the world's population live in countries where overweight and obesity kills more people than underweight. This happened because of the imbalance in the energy and calories consumed and the calories used by body.
